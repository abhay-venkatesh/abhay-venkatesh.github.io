Abhay most recently initiated the [frontier AI initiative](https://www.anduril.com/news/anduril-partners-with-openai-to-advance-u-s-artificial-intelligence-leadership-and-protect-u-s) at [Anduril](https://www.anduril.com/) to deploy and train frontier AI models for national security. Previously, he joined as one of the first AI hires at the company under CEO to [invent](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=Inp7zBgAAAAJ&citation_for_view=Inp7zBgAAAAJ:UeHWp8X0CEIC) [Lattice for Mission Autonomy](https://www.anduril.com/lattice/mission-autonomy), and built large parts of [Anduril's command-and-control platform and infrastructure](https://developer.anduril.com/guides/tasks/overview). He saw the company from less than 50 people to almost 10,000 and growing.

Before that, he was an AI researcher focusing on [machine learning](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=Inp7zBgAAAAJ&citation_for_view=Inp7zBgAAAAJ:qjMakFHDy7sC) and [machine learning systems](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=Inp7zBgAAAAJ&citation_for_view=Inp7zBgAAAAJ:9yKSN-GCB0IC) research, and was going to do his PhD at the [Carnegie Mellon School of Computer Science](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/) under the [Catalyst](https://catalyst.cs.cmu.edu/) machine learning and systems group. He worked on AI at places like [Meta](https://ai.meta.com/) and [Stanford](https://www.stanford.edu/research/).

Abhay is also a seed investor in startups like [Saronic](https://www.saronic.com/), [Mercor](https://www.mercor.com/), [Factory](https://factory.ai/), and more, and has worked with firms like [Greylock](https://greylock.com/), [Index](https://www.indexventures.com/), and [Sequoia](https://sequoiacap.com/) to deploy capital on their behalf.

Abhay wrote down his thoughts on startups in his [Startup Principles](https://x.com/AbhayVenkatesh1/status/1779250603191111983?s=20), his piece on [Sovereignty Gaps](https://x.com/AbhayVenkatesh1/status/2015827580696608949?s=20), and his piece on [The AI Economy](https://x.com/AbhayVenkatesh1/status/2060547068255748394?s=20).
